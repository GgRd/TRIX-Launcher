RAM settings
Define the RAM allocated to your game, a larger amount will increase performance.
It's important to avoid using too large a quantity, as this can lead to performance losses on your other applications.

-Maximum RAM: Indicates the total amount of RAM allocated to your game. It is recommended not to exceed 50% of the total RAM on your PC.

-Minimum RAM: Indicates the minimum amount of RAM allocated to start your game. We recommend that you set this value to no more than 4GB.
